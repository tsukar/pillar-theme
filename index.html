Profile

Ryotaro Tsukada was a master's student at Graduate School of Information Science and Technology, The University of Tokyo and a member of Kitsuregawa, Toyoda, Nemoto, Yoshinaga, Goda Lab.

His research interests are in information visualization and urban computing, especially congestion analysis and prediction for smart mobility.
